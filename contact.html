FAQ

How to cancel booking?

If you need to cancel a booking, please contact our support team through the contact form or call us directly at the provided numbers. We will assist you with the cancellation process.

When will I get the possession?

The possession date depends on the terms agreed upon during the purchase. Generally, possession is given once all payments are completed and necessary documents are signed.

Where can I pay the rent?

Rent payments can be made through our online portal, by bank transfer, or in person at our office. For more details, please contact our finance department.

How to contact the buyers?

You can contact buyers directly through our website's messaging system or by using the contact information provided in the listing details.

Why is my listing not showing up?

If your listing is not showing up, please ensure it has been approved by our team. If the problem persists, contact our support team for assistance.

How to promote my listing?

To promote your listing, you can use our premium listing services, which include featured placements, social media promotion, and email marketing. Contact us for more details.
